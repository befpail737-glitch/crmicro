CRMICRO Motor Drive Solutions

CRMICRO provides comprehensive power semiconductor solutions for BLDC, PMSM, and servo motor drives. Our portfolio combines high-efficiency power semiconductors with advanced motor control ICs to meet the demanding requirements of industrial and consumer applications.

Core Components

Power Inverter

CRMICRO's IGBTs and MOSFETs provide efficient 3-phase inverter stages for BLDC and PMSM control. Optimized for both low and high power applications with appropriate switching characteristics.

  • Efficient 3-phase inverter
  • Low switching losses
  • Thermal stability
CRMICRO IGBT Options

Motor Control

CRMICRO's dedicated motor control MCUs feature integrated FOC engines, ADCs, and PWM generators optimized for FOC and 6-step control algorithms.

  • Integrated FOC engine
  • Optimized PWM control
  • Fast ADC for feedback
CRMICRO MCU Options

Sensing & Feedback

CRMICRO's position sensors and signal conditioning ICs provide accurate rotor position feedback for precise motor control algorithms.

  • High-precision sensing
  • Robust feedback
  • Integrated signal conditioning
CRMICRO Sensor Options

Solution Advantages

High Efficiency

CRMICRO's power semiconductors achieve >97% inverter efficiency, reducing thermal management requirements and energy consumption.

Precise Control

Advanced motor control algorithms with field-oriented control (FOC) for smooth, quiet operation and optimal torque generation.

Reliability

Robust design and protection features ensure long-term reliability in harsh industrial environments.

Compact Design

High power density allows for compact motor drive solutions, reducing system footprint and material costs.
