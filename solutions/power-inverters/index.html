CRMICRO Power Inverter Solutions

CRMICRO provides comprehensive power semiconductor solutions for photovoltaic (PV) inverters and energy storage systems (ESS). Our portfolio combines high-efficiency power semiconductors with advanced control ICs to meet the demanding requirements of renewable energy applications.

Core Components

DC-DC Conversion

CRMICRO's SJ-MOS series provides excellent performance for boost conversion in PV applications. The optimized RDS(on) vs. Qg characteristics ensure high efficiency at the required switching frequencies.

  • High-efficiency boost conversion
  • MPPT optimization
  • Low EMI characteristics
CRMICRO MOSFET Options

DC-AC Inversion

For the main inverter stage, CRMICRO's IGBTs and SiC MOSFETs provide reliable power conversion with excellent efficiency and thermal performance.

  • High-power capability
  • Reliable operation
  • Thermal stability
CRMICRO IGBT Options

System Control

CRMICRO's MCU and power management ICs provide comprehensive system control, enabling features like MPPT algorithms, grid protection, and communication.

  • Advanced MPPT algorithms
  • Grid protection
  • Smart communication
