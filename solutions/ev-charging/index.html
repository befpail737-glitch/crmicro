CRMICRO EV Charging Solutions

CRMICRO provides comprehensive power semiconductor solutions for electric vehicle charging applications, including On-Board Chargers (OBC) and DC Fast Charging systems. Our portfolio combines high-efficiency power semiconductors with advanced control ICs to meet the demanding requirements of the EV market.

Core Components

PFC Stage

CRMICRO's SJ-MOS series provides excellent performance for PFC boost converters in OBC applications. The optimized RDS(on) vs. Qg characteristics ensure high efficiency at the required switching frequencies.

  • High-efficiency boost conversion
  • Optimized for continuous conduction mode
  • Low EMI characteristics
CRMICRO MOSFET Options

DC-DC Conversion

For LLC resonant converters, CRMICRO's SiC MOSFETs and GaN HEMTs enable high-frequency operation with minimal switching losses, resulting in high power density solutions.

  • High-frequency operation
  • Zero voltage switching
  • Compact design
CRMICRO SiC & GaN Options

System Control

CRMICRO's MCU and power management ICs provide comprehensive system control, enabling features like adaptive charging algorithms and safety protection.

  • Integrated protection features
  • Communication interfaces
  • Adaptive control
CRMICRO MCU Options

Solution Advantages

High Efficiency

CRMICRO's power semiconductors achieve >95% efficiency in OBC applications, reducing thermal management requirements and improving vehicle range.

Power Density

Advanced packaging and high-frequency operation allow for compact OBC designs, critical for space-constrained EV applications.

Reliability

Robust design and protection features ensure long-term reliability in harsh automotive environments.

Cost Effectiveness

Optimized device characteristics and system-level solutions reduce overall BOM costs without compromising performance.
