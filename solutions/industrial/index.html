Industrial Applications with CRMicro Components

The industrial sector demands robust, efficient, and reliable power semiconductor solutions for a wide range of applications. CRMicro power semiconductors provide the performance, durability, and efficiency required for harsh industrial environments with 24/7 operation requirements.

Key Industrial Applications

CRMicro offers a comprehensive portfolio of power semiconductors specifically designed for industrial applications. Our components meet the stringent requirements for reliability, temperature range, and robustness required in industrial environments.

Motor Drive Systems

CRMicro components are essential for variable frequency drives (VFDs), servo drives, and other motor control applications. Our IGBTs and power MOSFETs provide high efficiency and precise control for various motor types.

Industrial Power Supplies

From switched-mode power supplies (SMPS) to uninterruptible power supplies (UPS), CRMicro power semiconductors enable high-efficiency power conversion with excellent thermal performance and reliability.
