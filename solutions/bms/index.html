CRMICRO BMS Solutions

CRMICRO provides comprehensive Battery Management System (BMS) solutions for Energy Storage Systems (ESS), Electric Vehicles (EV), and portable applications. Our portfolio combines high-precision monitoring ICs with advanced power semiconductors to ensure battery safety, maximize performance, and optimize battery life.

Core Components

Cell Monitoring

CRMICRO's high-precision cell monitoring ICs provide accurate voltage, temperature, and current measurement for each cell in the battery pack. Multiple channel support for various pack configurations.

  • High-precision measurement
  • Multiple cell support
  • Temperature monitoring
CRMICRO BMS IC Options

Protection & Control

CRMICRO's power management ICs and MOSFETs provide protection functions and control algorithms to ensure safe operation of the battery pack under various conditions.

  • Over/under voltage protection
  • Overcurrent protection
  • Thermal management
CRMICRO Protection FET Options

System Control

CRMICRO's MCUs provide the computational power needed for complex battery management algorithms, communication protocols, and system-level control.

  • Advanced algorithms
  • Communication interfaces
  • State estimation
CRMICRO MCU Options

Solution Advantages

High Precision

CRMICRO's BMS ICs achieve measurement accuracy of ±2mV for cell voltages, ensuring optimal battery performance and longevity.

Safety First

Comprehensive protection algorithms prevent overcharge, over-discharge, overcurrent, and thermal runaway conditions.

Longevity

Advanced balancing algorithms extend battery life by maintaining uniform cell states across the entire pack.

Flexibility

Versatile solutions support various battery chemistries, configurations, and application requirements.
