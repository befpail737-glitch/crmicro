CRMicro Automotive Solutions

Advanced power semiconductor solutions for electric vehicles, powertrain systems, and battery management.

Automotive Applications with CRMicro Components

The automotive industry is rapidly evolving with the adoption of electric vehicles (EVs), advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS), and electrification of traditional systems. CRMicro power semiconductors provide the performance, reliability, and efficiency required for these demanding automotive applications.

Key Automotive Applications

CRMicro offers a comprehensive portfolio of power semiconductors specifically designed for automotive applications. Our components meet the stringent requirements for reliability, temperature range, and robustness required in automotive environments.

Electric Vehicle (EV) Systems

CRMicro components play a critical role in various EV systems including on-board chargers (OBC), DC/DC converters, inverter systems, and battery management systems. Our MOSFETs and IGBTs provide high efficiency, power density, and reliability for electric propulsion systems.

Powertrain Systems

From traction inverters to auxiliary systems, CRMicro power semiconductors enable high-efficiency power conversion and motor control in electric and hybrid powertrains. Our solutions optimize system performance while minimizing energy losses.
