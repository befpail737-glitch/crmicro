Energy Applications with CRMicro Components

The renewable energy sector demands high efficiency, reliability, and robust power semiconductor solutions for solar inverters, energy storage systems, and other clean energy applications. CRMicro power semiconductors provide the performance required for challenging environmental conditions and long-term operation in harsh environments.

Key Energy Applications

CRMicro offers a comprehensive portfolio of power semiconductors specifically designed for energy applications. Our components meet the stringent requirements for efficiency, reliability, and temperature performance required in renewable energy systems.

Solar Inverter Systems

CRMicro components are essential for various solar inverter topologies including string inverters, central inverters, and power optimizers. Our IGBTs, SiC MOSFETs, and power ICs provide high efficiency and long-term reliability.

Energy Storage Systems

From residential battery systems to grid-scale storage, CRMicro power semiconductors enable efficient bidirectional power conversion and battery management in energy storage applications.
