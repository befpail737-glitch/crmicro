CRMicro MOSFET Portfolio

Comprehensive range of power MOSFETs: SGT low-voltage, Super Junction high-voltage, and Planar technologies.

CRMicro MOSFETs: Advanced Power Solutions

CRMicro offers a comprehensive portfolio of power MOSFETs designed for a wide range of applications, from low-voltage battery-powered devices to high-voltage industrial systems. Our MOSFETs feature advanced trench and super junction technologies that deliver superior performance in terms of on-resistance, switching speed, and thermal characteristics.

CRMicro MOSFET Series Overview

SGT (Shielded Gate Trench) Low-Voltage MOSFETs

CRMicro's SGT MOSFETs utilize shielded gate trench technology to achieve exceptionally low on-resistance (RDS(on)) and gate charge (Qg), making them ideal for high-efficiency power conversion in battery-powered and portable applications.

SJ (Super Junction) High-Voltage MOSFETs

Super Junction MOSFETs provide an optimal balance of low on-resistance and fast switching performance, making them perfect for PFC circuits, adapters, and high-power industrial applications.

Planar MOSFETs

Traditional planar technology MOSFETs offer robust performance for applications requiring high reliability and stable characteristics over temperature and operating conditions.
