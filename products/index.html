CRMicro Product Solutions

As an authorized CRMicro distributor, we provide the full range of CRMicro products designed for power electronics applications. Our portfolio includes:

  • MOSFETs - From low-voltage SGT series to high-voltage Super Junction (SJ) MOSFETs
  • IGBTs - Discrete devices and power modules for industrial and automotive applications
  • Wide Bandgap Semiconductors - Silicon Carbide (SiC) and Gallium Nitride (GaN) solutions
  • Power Management ICs - PMICs, battery management, and motor driver ICs
  • Smart Sensors - MEMS sensors and other sensing technologies
  • Microcontrollers - MCUs for embedded control applications
