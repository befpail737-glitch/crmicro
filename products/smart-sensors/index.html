CRMicro Smart Sensors: Precision Sensing Solutions

CRMicro's smart sensor portfolio encompasses MEMS sensors, photoelectric sensors, and other intelligent sensing technologies designed for precision measurement and control applications. Our sensors provide high accuracy, reliability, and low power consumption across a wide range of environmental conditions.

CRMicro Smart Sensor Series Overview

MEMS Sensors

Miniaturized sensors using Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems technology. CRMicro offers accelerometers, gyroscopes, magnetometers, and integrated IMUs for applications requiring precise motion detection and control.

Photoelectric Sensors

Light-based sensors that detect objects, measure distances, or determine presence/absence. CRMicro's photoelectric sensors offer high reliability and precision for industrial automation and consumer applications.

Specialized Sensors

Application-specific sensors for temperature, humidity, pressure, and other environmental parameters. Engineered for harsh conditions and long-term stability.

As an authorized CRMicro distributor, we provide complete technical documentation, application notes, and engineering support for all CRMicro Smart Sensor products. Our Field Application Engineers can help you implement optimal sensor solutions in your designs.
