CRMicro Smart Controllers: Embedded Intelligence Solutions

CRMicro's smart controller portfolio encompasses a wide range of microcontrollers (MCUs) designed for embedded system applications. Our MCUs provide the processing power, connectivity, and analog peripherals required for intelligent control across various industries.

CRMicro Smart Controller Series Overview

8-bit MCUs

Cost-effective solutions for simple control applications requiring minimal processing power but reliable operation. Ideal for consumer electronics and basic industrial controls.

32-bit ARM MCUs

High-performance solutions based on ARM architecture, featuring advanced peripherals, connectivity options, and processing capabilities for complex applications.

Specialized Controllers

Application-specific microcontrollers with integrated peripherals tailored for particular functions such as motor control, IoT, or automotive applications.

As an authorized CRMicro distributor, we provide complete technical documentation, development tools, and engineering support for all CRMicro Smart Controller products. Our Field Application Engineers can help you select and implement the optimal controller solution for your embedded applications.
