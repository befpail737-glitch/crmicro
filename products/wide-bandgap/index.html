CRMicro Wide Bandgap Semiconductors: Next-Generation Power Solutions

CRMicro's wide bandgap semiconductor portfolio leverages Silicon Carbide (SiC) and Gallium Nitride (GaN) technologies to deliver superior performance in power conversion applications. These devices offer significant advantages over traditional silicon technologies, including higher switching frequencies, improved efficiency, and enhanced thermal performance.

CRMicro Wide Bandgap Series Overview

Silicon Carbide (SiC) Diodes

CRMicro's SiC diodes provide zero reverse recovery charge and excellent temperature stability, ideal for PFC circuits and high-frequency power supplies where efficiency and thermal management are critical.

Silicon Carbide (SiC) MOSFETs

High-performance SiC MOSFETs offering low on-resistance, fast switching, and superior temperature characteristics for demanding power conversion applications.

Gallium Nitride (GaN) Devices

Ultra-fast GaN devices for high-frequency applications where size, efficiency, and power density are paramount.

As an authorized CRMicro distributor, we provide complete technical documentation, application notes, and engineering support for all CRMicro wide bandgap products. Our Field Application Engineers can help you leverage the full potential of these advanced technologies in your designs.
