CRMicro IGBTs: Advanced Power Solutions

CRMicro offers a comprehensive portfolio of Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistors (IGBTs) designed for a wide range of applications, from motor drives to power conversion systems. Our IGBTs feature advanced trench and field-stop technologies that deliver superior performance in terms of switching speed, conduction losses, and thermal characteristics.

CRMicro IGBT Series Overview

Single IGBTs

CRMicro's discrete IGBTs provide robust performance for applications requiring individual device control. Available in various voltage and current ratings to match specific application requirements.

Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs)

Highly integrated modules combining IGBTs with driver circuits and protection functions, ideal for motor control applications requiring compact designs and enhanced system reliability.

Power Integrated Modules (PIMs)

Complete power stage solutions integrating multiple power devices with driver circuits in a single module, providing system-level integration for complex power conversion applications.

As an authorized CRMicro distributor, we provide complete technical documentation, selection guides, and engineering support for all CRMicro IGBT products. Our Field Application Engineers can help you select the optimal IGBT for your specific application requirements.
