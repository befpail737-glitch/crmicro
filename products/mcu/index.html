Technical Note from our FAE

CRMICRO's MCUs are designed with ARM Cortex-M cores for optimal balance of performance and power efficiency. The motor control MCUs include specialized peripherals like hardware-based FOC engines to reduce CPU load. The power management MCUs feature dedicated digital power control peripherals for high-efficiency power conversion applications.
