CRMicro Power ICs: Intelligent Power Control Solutions

CRMicro offers a comprehensive portfolio of power management integrated circuits designed to efficiently control and regulate power in electronic systems. Our Power ICs include Power Management ICs (PMICs), Battery Management System (BMS) ICs, and Motor Driver ICs that deliver high efficiency, precision, and reliability.

CRMicro Power IC Series Overview

Power Management ICs (PMICs)

CRMicro's PMIC portfolio includes DC-DC converters, LDO regulators, AC-DC controllers, and multi-rail power management solutions. These ICs feature high efficiency, low noise, and comprehensive protection functions.

Battery Management (BMS) ICs

Specialized ICs for monitoring, protecting, and optimizing battery systems in applications ranging from portable devices to electric vehicles. Features include cell balancing, protection circuits, and precise voltage/current measurement.

Motor Driver ICs

Complete solutions for controlling various motor types including BLDC, stepper, and brushed DC motors. CRMicro motor drivers provide high efficiency, low noise operation, and comprehensive protection features.

As an authorized CRMicro distributor, we provide complete technical documentation, reference designs, and engineering support for all CRMicro Power IC products. Our Field Application Engineers can help you implement optimal power management solutions in your designs.
