CRMICRO MOSFETs

As an official authorized distributor, we offer the complete range of CRMICRO MOSFETs. Our selection includes SGT (Shielded Gate Trench), SJ-MOS (Super Junction MOS), and Planar MOS series. Each product line is designed for specific applications, from low-voltage power management to high-voltage power conversion.

Product Series

SGT (Shielded Gate Trench) MOSFETs

Advanced trench technology with shielded gate structure for superior RDS(on) vs. Qg performance. Ideal for synchronous rectification in LLC converters and other high-frequency applications.

  • Low RDS(on) for improved efficiency
  • Optimized for high-frequency operation
  • Excellent thermal performance
View SGT Models

SJ-MOS (Super Junction) MOSFETs

High-voltage MOSFETs with breakthrough super junction technology. Perfect for PFC circuits, adapters, and industrial power supplies.

  • Ultra-low on-resistance at high voltages
  • Superior switching performance
  • Enhanced avalanche ruggedness
View SJ-MOS Models

Planar MOSFETs

Traditional planar technology MOSFETs for cost-effective solutions in applications where performance requirements are moderate.

  • Cost-effective solution
  • Robust performance
  • Wide range of voltage options
View Planar Models
