Products & Technology

What types of products does CRMicro offer?

CRMicro offers a comprehensive portfolio of power semiconductors including MOSFETs (SGT Low-Voltage, SJ-MOS High-Voltage, Planar), IGBTs (Single IGBTs, IPM Modules, PIM Modules), Wide Bandgap devices (SiC Diodes/MOSFETs, GaN), Power ICs (Power Management, Battery Management BMS, Motor Drivers), Smart Sensors (MEMS Sensors, Photoelectric Sensors), and Smart Controllers (MCUs).

What is the difference between SGT and SJ MOSFET technologies?

SGT (Shielded Gate Trench) MOSFETs offer the lowest on-resistance (RDS(on)) and gate charge (Qg) for low-voltage applications (typically 20V-100V). Super Junction (SJ) MOSFETs provide an optimal balance of low on-resistance and fast switching performance for high-voltage applications (typically 500V-800V).

What are the advantages of SiC MOSFETs compared to silicon devices?

Silicon Carbide (SiC) MOSFETs offer several key advantages over traditional silicon devices: Higher switching frequencies, lower switching losses, superior thermal conductivity, higher temperature operation, and lower on-state resistance. These benefits enable smaller, lighter, and more efficient power systems.

CRMicro components are available in standard industry packages including TO-220, TO-247, DPAK, D2PAK, SO-8, and various surface mount packages. High-power devices are available in modules with industry-standard pinouts. Contact us for specific packaging information for your component.
