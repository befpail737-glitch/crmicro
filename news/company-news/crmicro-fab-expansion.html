CRMICRO Announces New 12-inch Wafer Fabrication Capacity

CRMICRO News Team October 15, 2025 Company News

CRMICRO's new 12-inch wafer fabrication facility will significantly increase production capacity for power semiconductors

CRMICRO today announced a major expansion of its manufacturing capacity with the construction of a new 12-inch wafer fabrication facility. This strategic investment, valued at over $2 billion, will significantly increase the company's production capacity for power semiconductors to meet the rapidly growing demand in the global market.

Strategic Capacity Expansion

The new 12-inch fab will be located alongside CRMICRO's existing facilities and is expected to reach full production capacity by 2026. The facility will focus on advanced power semiconductor technologies including silicon carbide (SiC), trench MOSFETs, and IGBTs for automotive, industrial, and consumer applications.

"This investment demonstrates CRMICRO's commitment to meeting the growing demand for power semiconductors across all key markets," said the CEO of CRMICRO. "The new 12-inch facility will enhance our ability to serve customers with improved lead times and competitive pricing while maintaining our high standards for quality and reliability."

Technology Focus

The new facility will implement the latest manufacturing technologies optimized for power semiconductor production. Key capabilities will include:

  • Advanced trench MOSFET manufacturing processes
  • Super junction technology for high-voltage applications
  • SiC device processing capabilities
  • Automotive-qualified product lines

Market Impact

The expanded capacity is expected to address the ongoing supply constraints in the power semiconductor market. This investment positions CRMICRO to better serve customers in rapidly growing markets including electric vehicles, renewable energy systems, and data center power supplies.

Initial production from the new facility will focus on high-demand product families including 650V and 1200V super junction MOSFETs for PFC and power adapter applications. The facility will gradually expand to include additional product lines throughout 2026.

Environmental Considerations

The new facility incorporates advanced environmental controls and energy-efficient processes to minimize environmental impact. Water recycling systems and waste heat recovery will be implemented to reduce resource consumption.

Timeline and Milestones

Key milestones for the facility include:

  • Q4 2025: Completion of facility construction
  • Q1 2026: Equipment installation begins
  • Q2 2026: Initial product qualifications
  • Q4 2026: Full production capacity achieved

This capacity expansion reinforces CRMICRO's position as a leading supplier of power semiconductors and demonstrates the company's commitment to long-term growth in the power electronics market.
