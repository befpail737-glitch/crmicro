About CRMicro Distributor

Your trusted partner for authentic CRMicro components and technical expertise.

Our Commitment to Excellence

As an authorized CRMicro distributor, we are committed to providing genuine CRMicro power semiconductors alongside expert technical support. Founded in 2010, our company has grown to become a trusted partner for engineers and procurement teams worldwide seeking reliable access to CRMicro products.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between CRMicro's innovative power semiconductor solutions and global customers who need authentic components with comprehensive technical support. We focus on quality, authenticity, and technical excellence to support your design and manufacturing processes.

Our Expertise

Our team includes experienced Field Application Engineers (FAEs) with deep expertise in power semiconductor technologies. We understand the challenges designers face when implementing power solutions and provide practical, effective support throughout the design process.

With our global network and local support, we ensure that customers receive not only authentic CRMicro components but also the technical knowledge needed to implement them successfully in their applications.
