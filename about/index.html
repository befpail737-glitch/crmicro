About LiTong Electronics

LiTong Electronics is an officially authorized distributor for CR Micro (CRMICRO), China's leading power semiconductor manufacturer. We specialize in providing high-quality power semiconductors including MOSFETs, IGBTs, SiC/GaN devices, power ICs, sensors, and MCUs to customers worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide customers with the highest quality power semiconductor solutions while delivering exceptional technical support and service. We aim to bridge the gap between innovative CRMICRO technology and global market needs.

Company History

Founded with the vision of bringing advanced Chinese semiconductor technology to global markets, LiTong Electronics has grown to become a trusted partner for engineers and designers worldwide. Our journey began with a focus on power MOSFETs and has expanded to include the complete CRMICRO product portfolio.
